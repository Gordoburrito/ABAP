# **Mopar Product Formatting Guidelines**

### **General Rules:**
1. **Mopar Capitalization:** Always use "Mopar," not "MOPAR."
2. **Mopar Cars Include:** Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge, and Plymouth.
3. **Year Ranges:** Use the earliest and latest applicable years for a product.
4. **Vehicle Makes:** List **all applicable makes** instead of just "Mopar Cars."
5. **Vintage vs. Classic:**
   - **Vintage Mopar Car** → For **1949 or earlier** models.
   - **Classic Mopar Car** → For **1950 and later** models.
   - **Vintage & Classic Mopar Cars** → When applicable to both.

---

### **Meta Title Formatting (Max 70 Characters)**
- **Start with Product Name.**
- **Include Correct Year(s).**
- **Use a Short, Clear Title.**
- **Specify Vehicle Makes, Not Just Mopar Cars.**
- **Use Proper Spacing for Dashes.**
- **Include Product Category at the End (if it fits).**

#### **Examples:**
✅ **Cowl Vent Gasket for 1939 Chrysler, DeSoto, and Plymouth**  
✅ **Cowl Vent Gasket for 1928 - 1961 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth**  
✅ **Cowl Vent Gasket for 1950 Dodge Trucks | Weatherstripping**

---

### **If the Title Exceeds 70 Characters, Shorten in This Order:**
1. **Remove Spaces Between Year Ranges:**  
   - 1928 - 1961 → 1928-1961
2. **Replace "and" with "&":**  
   - Chrysler, Dodge, and Plymouth → Chrysler, Dodge & Plymouth
3. **Remove the Last Comma Before "&":**  
   - Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge, and Plymouth → Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth
4. **Remove the Product Category (if needed):**  
   - Cowl Vent Gasket for 1928-1961 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth | Weatherstripping  
   → Cowl Vent Gasket | 1928-1961 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth
5. **If Still Too Long, Remove "&" and Use a Clean List:**  
   - Cowl Vent Gasket | 1928-1961 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth  
   → Cowl Vent Gasket | 1928-1961 Chrysler DeSoto Dodge Plymouth
6. **As a Last Resort, Replace "for" with "|":**  
   - Cowl Vent Gasket for 1928-1961 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth  
   → Cowl Vent Gasket | 1928-1961 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth

---

### **Description Formatting (Max 160 Characters)**
1. **Must Include "Classic," "Vintage," or "Vintage & Classic Mopar Car."**
2. **List All Applicable Makes.**
3. **Include Year Before "Vintage/Classic."**
4. **Use Hyphen ("-") Instead of "to" for Year Ranges.**
5. **Keep It Short, Clear, and Concise.**
6. **If the Title Lacked the Category, Add It to the Description.**
7. **For Overly Long Descriptions:**
   - **Remove unnecessary commas.**
   - **Use "&" instead of "and."**
   - **Omit "Vintage Mopar Car" if implied.**
   - **Condense phrases while maintaining clarity.**

#### **Examples:**
✅ **Shop our one-piece formed cowl vent gasket for 1939 Vintage Mopar Cars - Chrysler, DeSoto & Plymouth. Easy install & reliable weather protection.**  
✅ **Secure your 1930-1948 Vintage Mopar Car - Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth with our Cowl Vent Gasket Rubber.**  
✅ **Upgrade your 1940-1948 Vintage Mopar Car (Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge, Plymouth) with our easy-install, durable cowl vent gasket.**  
✅ **Seal your Classic Mopar with a durable cowl vent gasket for 1940-1948 Chrysler, DeSoto, Dodge & Plymouth.**  

---

### **Final Notes:**
- Maintain **uniformity** in product names, descriptions, and categories.
- Use **structured, logical formatting** for readability.
- Follow **Google-friendly** character limits for SEO.
